Hello, my name is Michael D'Argenio. I am an electrical and computer engineer turned educator. I have a lot of experience in embedded software and hardware design. I am taking my technical experiences and leveraging them to create engaging curricula for students to interact with STEM and help make the world a better place.

This is my website I modeled after operating systems of the 90s. Please feel free to take a look around. To learn more about my past experiences and qualifications, check out my resume. For more information on my principles as an educator, check out my teaching philosophy. For some lesson plans and fun projects to build, check out my projects folder or my github. To have some fun, play around with some of my applications on the desktop.
